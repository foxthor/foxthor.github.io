What im about

I am a self taught web developer and aspiring indie game creator. I am learning to code and program using various free and paid resources I have found on the web. So far I have been learning HTML and CSS, bootstrap, JavaScript, Java and C# in Unity. Im also interested in Cyber Security, Ethical Hacking and the effects of cyber warfare and virtual criminal activity Including its political motivations, which will have a bigger impact on our lives as the future unravels. The whole I.T industry is very exciting at the moment, with technology rapidly evolving. I am interested in the new developments in machine learning and A.I. which poses some very large changes to our future. The internet is intertwined with all of our lives and is a facinating realm of possibilities that is ever evolving. We now more than ever, have the greatest tools at our fingertips to develop ideas and realize them.

I have been using resources such as Free Code Camp and Udemy , Ben Tristams Unity course helped me greatly in learning the basics of C# and programming fundamentals, while creating games as I learned. Other notable resources include, Udacity , Stack-Overflow , code academy, Youtube tutorials. Theres so many great resources out there and so much to learn at our fingertips.
